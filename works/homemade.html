HomeMade

September, 2021

Role

Freelance

Contribution

UI Design, UX Research, Prototype

HomeMade is an upcoming food app that users watch cooking video from a chef and being able to order the food easily.

The goal of this project is to helps users to order food easily from cooking videos.

The challenge is to make an understandable user flow to my clients and design a modern interfaces that make users want to stay longer.

Prototype

Competitive Research food apps that I pick for research are HungerStation, Uber Eats, and Talabat.

Research is a very crucial thing before proceed to design the HomeMade app, it allows me to easily explain to my clients about competitor weaknesses and strengths.

UI Kit is important when it comes to presentation, this UI components contains critical components such as Fonts, Colors, Icons, etc.
