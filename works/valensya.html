July, 2021

Role

Freelance

Contribution

UI Design, UX Research, Prototype

VALENSYA is a simple online fashion store app that makes shopping/finding fashion items easier and pleasant.

The goal of this project is to create an app prototype that easily helps users to follow fashion trends.

The challenge is to keep it simple but reliable, attract user trust, and make users not hesitate to give out their personal information.

Prototype

When making project like this, it always comes a Design Process, start from research then ideate, Competitive Research > Indentify Target Groups > User Flow > Wireframing and finally Prototyping.

Competitive Research is the first thing to start, since they are many online fashion app store, I pick Zalora, Berrybenka, and H&M to start the research.

Is important to understand how VALENSYA app compare to other competitors, evaluated competitors strength and weakness allow me to understand customer expectations.

Identify Target Groups is young people, age 19-40 years old. 66.7% Female and 33.3% Male.

UI Kit it contains critical UI components like Fonts, Colors, Icons, etc. is simple things to do but critical when presenting the presentation to your customers.

The idea is a simple and minimalist design, making sure that the Home & Navigation feature is convenient for the user while shopping.

Take only a few steps to join with quick registration that you can skip and do it later.

Fast & effective, Check-out is filled with only essential information, allowing customers to avoid confusion while they shopping.
